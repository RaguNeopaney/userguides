INTRODUCTION : Team Tangerines

Tangerines is a web page with a score on it and the game underneath. The game will have five pots at the bottom of the column and 5 rows of leaves making a 5x5 grid. The objective of the game will be to flip over the leaves and avoid flipping over a leaf that had a exploding(red) tangerine behind it. There will be clues on each of the pots and end of the rows that tell you the point value of the row and how many exploding tangerines are on each row. When flipping over a leaf (one square of the 5x5 grid) you will either get a red, green, orange, or gold tangerine. Red tangerines end the game. Green tangerines are worth 1, orange tangerines are worth 2 and gold tangerines will be worth 3. The points will be multiplicative, as every time you get a tangerine, it will be multiplied to your current score.

About the game

The goal of Tangerines is to determine the location of orange, gold, and red tangerines on the game board. The player uses the hints provided by the numbers on the sides of the board to deduce which tiles have the desired value and which are bombs. The player scores points by flipping the correct tiles, with the point value multiplied by the number on the tile. The objective is to score as many points as possible by avoiding the bombs and uncovering the valuable tiles. To flip a leaf, click on it. In Tangerines, players can use the memo function to keep track of the leaves they have already flipped and the value they have deduced for each tile. The memo function allows players to mark tiles with different symbols or colors, such as a flag for exploding tangerines or an X for tiles that have already been flipped.

How to play

    Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use the memo in Tangerines:

  1. Select the leaf you want to mark: Use the cursor to select the leaf you want to mark and press the memo button to bring up the memo function.
  2. Choose a symbol or color: Use the direction buttons or touch screen to select the desired symbol or color that you want to use to mark the tile.
  3. Apply the mark: Press the symbol on the screen to apply the mark to the tile.
  4. Repeat the process: Repeat the process for each tile you want to mark, as many times as necessary.

FAQ'S

What is Tangerines?

Tangerines is a game that involves flipping over tiles to uncover the value of each tile and score points.

How do you play Tangerines?

The player uses the numbers on the sides of the board to deduce which tiles have a value of 2x or 3x and which are explosive tangerines. Points are scored by flipping the correct tiles, with the point value multiplied by the number on the tile.

How do you get hints in Tangerines?

The hints are provided by the numbers on the sides of the board, which indicate the sum of the tiles in a row or column and how many explosive tangerines are present in that row/column.

What happens if you flip a explosive tangerine in Tangerines?

If you flip an explosive tangerine in Tangerines, you will lose all your coins from the current level and risk going down to a lower level.

What is the objective of Tangerines?

The objective of Tangerines is to score as many points as possible by uncovering the valuable tiles and avoiding the bombs.
